The Real Problem

Most businesses don't have a pipeline problem. They have a bandwidth problem.

The people best placed to win new business are the same people running the operation, servicing clients, and keeping everything moving. There is simply no room left for consistent prospecting.

So outreach becomes reactive — sporadic bursts when the pipeline looks thin, followed by long stretches of silence. The result is a feast-and-famine cycle that never quite resolves itself.

Outrion exists to break that cycle. A systematic, always-on outreach operation running in the background while your team focuses on what it does best.

02

03
One-touch outreach does not convert Sporadic messages rarely land. Consistent, personalised, multi-touch sequences do.

No system, no accountability Without a dedicated process and team behind it, new business development will always fall to the bottom of the list.

The Thinking Behind Outrion

Built from lived experience.

Former insurance broking professional. Harrison built the system he always wished existed.

Harrison spent years working inside UK insurance broking. Not in theory. Inside it — watching how independent firms operate, where their time actually goes, and what stops good businesses from growing the way they should.

The pattern was the same everywhere. Brilliant operators, a strong book of business, genuine expertise. And a new business pipeline that was permanently underfunded because the people who should be prospecting were always the people most needed elsewhere. Renewals, client queries, compliance, operations. The diary filled up before new business got a look in.

"Every independent broker I worked alongside faced the same problem: great at the job, no time to find the next client. I built Outrion to solve that."

Harrison founded Outrion to close that gap. Not as a generic outreach tool, but as a system built specifically around the commercial reality of UK independent professionals. The sector knowledge, the buyer psychology, the compliance considerations, the language that actually gets replies in these markets — it is all embedded in how the Outrion team approaches every campaign.

The result is an outreach operation that slots in seamlessly alongside your existing business. No disruption, no learning curve, no time investment beyond a 60-minute onboarding call. Your pipeline gets the attention it has always deserved. Your team gets to focus on closing.

FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before booking a call.

What does "done for you" actually mean — how much time does this take from us?
Minimal. You will spend roughly 60 minutes in the first week on an onboarding call and reviewing your outreach sequences before they go live. After that, your only involvement is attending the meetings we book and reading the Friday report if you want visibility on activity. Our team handles everything in between.
How long before we see results?
We are live within 5 working days of signing. Most clients see the first positive replies in weeks two and three as sequences gain traction. Meetings typically start landing toward the end of the first month or into month two as the pipeline matures. Every campaign is different depending on market, ICP, and message fit.
What is the guarantee?
If you don't receive at least 3 warm replies or 1 booked meeting in your first 30 days, month two is on us. No questions asked, no small print. We back the system with a concrete commitment because we are confident it delivers, and because our clients deserve accountability rather than excuses.
Is cold outreach compliant in regulated sectors?
Yes. All outreach is conducted under legitimate interest provisions and in line with GDPR and UK data protection requirements. We do not use purchased contact databases. Every prospect is researched using publicly available information. All copy is reviewed and approved by you before anything is sent, so you maintain full visibility and control.
You reached us through outreach — is that not the proof?
Exactly. If you received a message from Outrion and followed it here, you have already experienced the system in action. The same precision-built outreach process we run for our clients is the one that found you. That is not a coincidence. It is the product demonstration.
What if we want to cancel?
30 days notice, in writing. No long-term contracts, no penalty fees. We want clients who stay because the pipeline is working, not because they are locked into something they cannot exit.
